Ransomeware

:( Alert…!!! Ransomware :(

Readers stay alert whenever they do something or download from the internet that you think is not good. Never download such things coz Ransomware is on its way.

Hii, I am Prinal, writing this blog on a real story about a crucial ransomware attack. This happened with one of my friends last week. He approached me to format his laptop. We did this in the afternoon however after rebooting it was working so fine.
By evening he gave me a call, in despite he was so shocked and threatened, and asked me to come over as soon as possible. By some time when I reached home, he asked me to check his laptop where his files were locked, encrypted with a password.
We couldn't open it, but we find there a readme.txt file that was supposed to be not there. We opened it, it was really shocking for us to see that ransomware has been installed on his laptop.



This was the message from that readme.txt file. The name of ransomware is ORKF Ransomware which was supposedly downloaded from a popular website that has cracked software's i.e., getintopc.com

My friend downloaded a software named Adobe Photoshop which was just 11 MB and coincidentally he opened it which leads to persistent attacks on the encryption of his files.
Moreover, we searched for a decryption key on the nomoreransom.org website but we couldn't find it or else nowhere.

The final thing was just to format the entire laptop with files again. Where he lost all the workload which he created in the past 3 years. This blog is just for all readers to stay safe from this kind of suspicious link.

Things to remember are:

  • Never approach free software's from pirated websites like getintopc.com.
  • Whether you find it just check its properties and file size. If it's genuine then it's okay but always check.
  • And most important, install a good Antivirus (Quick Heal Total Security recommended) which will scan the bound app or software to any file if it's malicious.


"Never let yourself get hacked!!!"
